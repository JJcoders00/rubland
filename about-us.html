Our Story

Founded in 2005 by **Mr. Soy Vattapillil** in Thodupuzha, Rubland Industries began with a simple mission: to help the small-holder rubber farmer. At the time, over 80% of rubber in India was sold as low-grade sheets due to inconsistent sun-drying and inefficient traditional smokehouses.

"We bridged this gap by making professional-grade smoke houses affordable and accessible to every planter in Kerala."

Today, Rubland Industries is a name synonymous with trust and precision engineering in the rubber planting community. We replace guesswork with scientifically engineered solutions, ensuring every sheet produced meets international Grade-4 RSS quality standards.
