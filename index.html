Company Profile

Revolutionizing
Rubber Processing

Natural rubber in India is mainly produced by small holder farmers. Due to the lack of appropriate dryers, more than 80% of the total production has to be sold as low-grade Ribbed Smoked Sheet (RSS) for a relatively low price.

Rubland Industries bridges this gap. Smoking the sheets produces high-grade RSS, which sells for considerably more. Our smoke houses prevent oxidation and mould growth.
